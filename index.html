About Me

I am a biomedical engineer and neuroscientist with expertise in signal and image processing, machine learning, neuroimaging, neurofeedback, and brain stimulation techniques, including transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS), temporal interference (TI), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS). My research focuses on emotion regulation, pain relief, and decision-making, integrating brain stimulation with neuroimaging to elucidate neural mechanisms and optimize therapeutic interventions.
