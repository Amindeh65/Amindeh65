About Me

I am a biomedical engineer and neuroscientist with expertise in neurofeedback and brain stimulation, including transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS), temporal interference (TI), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS). My research focuses on emotion regulation, pain relief, and decision-making, combining brain stimulation with neuroimaging to understand neural mechanisms and optimize interventions.
