About Me

I am a biomedical engineer and neuroscientist specializing in neuroimaging (EEG, fMRI), neurofeedback, and non-invasive brain stimulation—transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS), temporal interference (TI), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS). My research targets neural mechanisms of emotion regulation, pain relief, and decision-making.

Currently, I’m a Postdoctoral Research Scholar in the Department of Psychological & Brain Sciences at Dartmouth College with Prof. Tor Wager, bridging engineering and neuroscience to develop scalable, translational interventions.
