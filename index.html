About Me

I am a biomedical engineer and neuroscientist with expertise signal and image processing, machine learning, neuro-imaging, neurofeedback and brain stimulation, including transcranial direct current stimulation (tDCS), temporal interference (TI), and transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS). My research focuses on emotion regulation, pain relief, and decision-making, combining brain stimulation with neuroimaging to understand neural mechanisms and optimize interventions.
